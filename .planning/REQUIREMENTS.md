# Project Requirements

## R1: Identity & Security (High Priority)
- **R1.1 Multi-Tenant Auth**: Users belong to one or more schools/campuses.
- **R1.2 RBAC Matrix**: Permissions managed by roles (SuperAdmin, Admin, Teacher).
- **R1.3 Data Isolation (RLS)**: No data leakage between campuses by default.
- **R1.4 Secure Routing**: UI only accessible based on authenticated roles.

## R2: Financial Management (High Priority)
- **R2.1 Flexible Fee Structures**: Support for one-time, recurring, and optional fees.
- **R2.2 Automated Invoicing**: Generation of fee dues on preset dates.
- **R2.3 Payment Tracking**: Clear status of paid/pending/overdue accounts.
- **R2.4 Reconciliation**: Financial reports for SuperAdmins across all campuses.

## R3: Academic Operations (Medium Priority)
- **R3.1 SIS Management**: Complete student/staff lifecycle (Admission to TC).
- **R3.2 Attendance**: Daily tracking for students and staff with audit logs.
- **R3.3 Examination Results**: Digital report card generation and grade management.

## R4: Engagement & Communications (Medium Priority)
- **R4.1 WhatsApp Integration**: Critical notifications (Fees, Attendance).
- **R4.2 Real-time Dashboard**: Live feed of school operations for principals.

## R5: Logistics (Low Priority)
- **R5.1 Transport Tracking**: Routes, bus assignation, and fee integration.
- **R5.2 Hostel Management**: Room allocation and basic facility tracking.
